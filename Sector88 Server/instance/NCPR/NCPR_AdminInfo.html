[[BUSHCRAFTING]]

Get plant fibre from harvesting plants and get stones by digging them up
- 5 bolts can be placed as a spike trap & covered with plant material || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 5
- pelts can be placed ( also human skin if gore addon is installed ) -> vanilla and HunterZ
- pelts + knife = neckwarmer ( also human skin if goreaddon is installed ) -> vanilla and HunterZ || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 1
- trophies from skinning various animals
- some trophies + rags/fabric = wearable trophies ( also skull if goreaddon is installed ) || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 1
- goathorn + tool = drinkinghorn || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 2
- bear skull & pig head are naturally wearable
- plant matrial + netting = plant ghillie || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 2
- bones + knife = bone needles, bone awl or carved hipbone || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 1
- bark or plant material + knife = plant fibre || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 1
- dark bark + water = bark paste || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 4
- bark paste + pelt = tanned leather || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 4
- tanned Leather + leather sewingkit = leather clothing || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 4
- fibre + bone needles = improvised sewingkit || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 1
- improvised sewingkit + bone awl = improvised leather sewingkit || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 1
- plant fibre in hands = rag || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 2
- plant fibre in hands = fibre rope || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 2
- fibre rope in hands = fibre belt || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 2
- fibre or rope + long or short wooden sticks = bushcraft kit / shelterkit || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 3
- treestump + tool = hollowed treestump stache || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 5
- bushcraft kit + small stone = improvised hammer || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 3
- bushcraft kit + stoneknife = improvised hatchet || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 3
- bushcraft kit + carved hipbone = improvised shovel or hatchet || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 3
- bushcraft kit + stone- or boneknife = stone- or bonespear || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 2
- bushcraft kit + stone- or boneknife = stone- or bonespear || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 2
- bushcraft kit + rope = slingshot ( ammo = small stones ) || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 5
- plantmaterial in hands = leafcrowns ( seasondependent ) || with optional Terje Skillmod from BC LVL 2
- rope/gutrope/fibrerope + small sticks = snaretrap


[[GORE]]

Get human heads, skin and infected meat by skinning zombies or humans
- human head + various Tools = skull pendant ( for holster slot/spike/trophyboard ), face skin, ear
- human face + burlapsack = zombie mask ( hides you from zombies when hands free, not running and not to close )
- firewood + knife = wooden head
- head + long stick = impaled head
- human skin + knife = skincape ( if bushcrafting installed )
- human skin + leather sewingkit = skintop/pants
- chainsaw + unconcious or tied up players = skin and quater alive

[[SCRAPMETAL]]

Get scrapmetal by useing a sharp tool to disassemble
metalplates, barrels, guns, metal tools, generators, car doors & other stuff.
- angle grinder( not craftable, powerpack rechargeable with battery charger )
- scrapmetal + blowtorch/angle grinder = sledgehammer, axe, knife, lamp,
foldable hatchet/shovel, barrel, tripod, firepit, gasoline canister, pliers, gardenpot, barrel, metalcrate, metalplates, visor, mask, pocketheater
- scrapmetal + pliers / screwdriver = nails, wire, mess tin, barbedwire, adjustable buttstock/handguard, crossbow bolt, fishinghook
- scrapmetal + wooden plank = playable banjo


[[SEWING]]

Get fabric by folding a stack of rags or tarp in your hands.
- fabric + sewingkit = medical-, grenade-, universal-, platecarrier pouches, knife sheath,
plain armbands/flags, backbag, holster, carrying strap, chestbag, armpouch, cape
- fabric + waterproofing spray = tarp
- fabric + knife = netting
- camonet + knife = camonet cloak/gunwrap

- planks + nails = plain signs, plain canvas, plain orgasign
- planks + nails = trophyboard ( takes wolfhead, bearskull, tusks, horns, antlers, foxtail, rabbithead, pighead, skull, featherhat, heads )

- fabric + sewingkit = improvised bandage ( only if sewing addon installed )
- burned out fires + knife = charcoal (single use)

[[VEHICLES]]

This addon allows the construction of a raft and a buggy
- set up a construction side with sticks and rope
- inspecting the discription text of the construction side gives further hints on crafting
- construction is only possible from outside the construction side
- Buggy Stage1: scrapmetal; Construction Tool: blowtorch/angle grinder
- Buggy Stage2: wooden planks, nails; Construction Tool: hammer
- Buggy Stage3: scrapmetal,flashlight, ducttape; Construction Tool: bare hands
- Buggy Stage4: scrapmetal, engine; Construction Tool: blowtorch/angle grinder
- Raft Stage1: scrapmetal; Construction Tool: blowtorch/angle grinder
- Raft Stage2: barrels, rope; Construction Tool: bare hands
- Raft Stage3: wooden planks & nails; Construction Tool: hammer
- Raft Stage4: scrapmetal, engine; Construction Tool: blowtorch/angle grinder
- power generator + tool = generator parts
- generator parts + scrapmetal = engine
- boat + tool = engine
- boat / any Wheel + tool = rubber
- rubber + scrapmetal = buggy wheel
