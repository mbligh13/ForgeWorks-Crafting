
		
		                   Material Gathering for Weapon Crafting
		
		
		Gathering these materials will help you to make some improvised weapons
		
		
Duct Tape
		
Wooden Log/Plank
		
Metal Pipe
		
Duct Tape
		
Hacksaw(any sharp object)
		
Screwdriver
		
Pliers
		
Pipe
		
MetalPlate or Barrels
		
Wooden Log or Plank
		
IWP Scrap Metal -- Hacksaw + Barrel/Metal Plate
		
IWP Scrap Spikes -- Hacksaw + Barrel/Metal Plate
		
IWP Spring -- Screw Driver/Pliers + Alarm Clock/Kitchen Timer
		
IWP Screws -- Screw Driver/Pliers + Alarm Clock/Kitchen Timer

IWP Gunpowder -- Ammo + Pliers
		
		
		
		
		
		
		
								Deadly Baseball Bats 
		
		
		These instructions will guide you to craft deadly baseball bats, very effective agains any enemy 
		
		
Barbed Baseballbat  --  Baseballbat + Barbed Wire 
		
Spiked Baseballbat  --  Baseballbat + 25 IWP Scrap Spikes 
		
		
		
		
		
		
								Single Shot Postol 
		
		
		These instructions will guide you to craft a single shot pistol that fires .357 rounds 
		
		
IWP Pistol Barrel  --  Hacksaw(any sharp object) + Pipe 
		
IWP Pistol Grip  --  5 IWP ScrapMetal + DuctTape 
		
IWP Pistol Body  --  IWP Pistol Barrel + IWP Pistol Grip 
		
IWP Pistol Trigger  --  5 IWP Scrap Metal + Pliers 
		
IWP Pistol COMPLETE  --  IWP Pistol Body + IWP Pistol Trigger 
		
		
		
		
		
		
								Double Barrel Shotgun 
		
		
		These instructions will guide you to craft a short double barrel shotgun. 
		
		
IWP Shotgun Barrel  --  IWP Pistol Barrel + IWP Pistol Barrel 
		
IWP Shotgun Barrel Shroud  --  Hacksaw(any sharp object) + Wooden Log/Wooden Plank 
		
IWP Shotgun Barrel Assembly  --  IWP Barrel Shroud + IWP Shotgun Barrel 
		
IWP Shotgun Grip  --  Hacksaw(any sharp object) + Wooden Log/Wooden Plank 
		
IWP Shotgun Triggers  --  10 IWP Scrap Metal + Pliers 
		
IWP Shotgun Frame  --  IWP Shotgun Grip + 15 IWP Scrap Metal 
		
IWP Shotgun Frame Assembly  --  IWP Shotgun Frame + IWP Shotgun Triggers 
		
IWP Shotgun COMPLETE  --  IWP Barrel Assembly + IWP Shotgun Frame Assembly 
		
		
		
		
		
		
								Sub Machine Gun 
		
		
			These instructions will guide you to craft a sub machine gun and magazine, effective at short range. Fires .380 rounds. 
		
		
IWP SMG Barrel  --  Hacksaw + Pipe 
		
IWP SMG Bolt  --  5 IWP Scrap Metal + 1 IWP Spring 
		
IWP SMG Barrel Assembly  --  IWP SMG Barrel + IWP SMG Bolt 
		
IWP SMG Grip  --  Hacksaw(any sharp object) + Wooden Log/Wooden Plank 
		
IWP SMG Frame  --  10 IWP Screws + 15 IWP Scrap Metal 
		
IWP SMG Frame Assembly  --  IWP SMG Frame + IWP SMG Grip 
		
IWP SMG COMPLETE  --  IWP Barrel Assembly + IWP SMG Frame Assembly 
		
IWP SMG Magazine  --  10 IWP Scrap Metal + IWP Spring 




							Pistol Shotgun


			These instructions will guide you to craft a pistol shotgun. Fires any shotgun round.



IWP Pistol Shotgun Barrel -- Hacksaw + Pipe


IWP Pistol Shotgun Frame -- 15 IWP Scrap Metal + Pliers


IWP Pistol Shotgun Frame Assembly -- IWP Pistol Shotgun Frame + 10 IWP Screws


IWP Pistol Shotgun Trigger (and Hammer) -- 5 IWP Scrap Metal + Hacksaw


IWP Pistol Shotgun Body -- IWP Pistol Shotgun Frame Assembly + IWP Pistol Shotgun Barrel


IWP Pistol Shotgun COMPLETE -- IWP Pistol Shotgun Body + IWP Pistol Shotgun Trigger

IWP Bolt Action Rifle Body -- IWP Bolt Action Rifle Chamber Complete + IWP Bolt Action Rifle Barrel

IWP Bolt Action Rifle Short (Complete) -- IWP Bolt Action Rifle Body + IWP Bolt Action Rifle Grip
